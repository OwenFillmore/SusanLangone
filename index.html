About Susan

Susan Langone is a multifaceted artist, designer, and mental health advocate whose work bridges the worlds of creative expression and therapeutic practice.

As the Director of The Center For Resiliency, Susan has dedicated her career to helping individuals and communities heal through evidence-based therapeutic practices, creative arts therapies, and compassionate care.

Mission

To empower individuals to discover their inner strength and resilience through creative expression, personalized care, and supportive community connections.

Susan's artistic practice encompasses commissioned paintings, interior design consultations, and transformative installations that bring warmth, color, and human connection to living spaces.

Her unique approach combines professional training in mental health services with a deep passion for visual arts, resulting in work that is both aesthetically beautiful and emotionally restorative.
